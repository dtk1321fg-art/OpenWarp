## Rules

1. Do not claim warp drive is solved.
2. Do not present speculation as proof.
3. Bring sources when making scientific claims.
4. Separate theory, experiment, and opinion.
5. Be skeptical without being toxic.
6. No weapons development or harmful applications.
7. No spam, scams, or fake breakthroughs.
8. Keep the mission focused on peaceful spacetime-engineering research and human expansion beyond Earth.

## Research Standard

A strong Discord post should answer:

- What is the claim?
- What source supports it?
- What assumptions does it make?
- What could disprove it?
- How does it connect to OpenWarp?
