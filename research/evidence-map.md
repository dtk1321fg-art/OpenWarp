# Exotic Stress-Energy Evidence Map

This file tracks evidence for exotic or negative stress-energy-like phenomena.

The goal is not to prove warp drive immediately. The goal is to separate:

1. What is experimentally real
2. What is theoretically allowed
3. What is controllable
4. What is scalable
5. What could matter gravitationally

## Evidence Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 0 | Speculative claim only |
| 1 | Mathematical possibility |
| 2 | Accepted theoretical effect |
| 3 | Experimental evidence exists |
| 4 | Reproducible controlled experiment |
| 5 | Tunable and shapeable |
| 6 | Scalable stress-energy engineering candidate |
| 7 | Measurable spacetime coupling |

## Candidate Effects

| Candidate | Evidence Level | Why it matters | Main weakness |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Casimir effect | 3 | Real vacuum-boundary effect; possible negative energy region in theory | Tiny; geometry/material issues; not automatically useful for warp |
| Squeezed quantum states | 2-3 | Can suppress vacuum fluctuations in some observables | Effects are small and tightly constrained |
| Quantum field local negative energy | 2 | QFT allows local negative energy under special conditions | Quantum inequalities restrict amount/duration |
| Alcubierre exotic matter | 1 | Directly relevant to original warp metric | No known usable source at required scale |
| Positive-energy sublight warp models | 1-2 | May avoid some exotic matter assumptions for sublight cases | Does not solve FTL; critiques remain |
| Modified gravity routes | 1 | Could alter requirements in non-standard theories | Must still match observations and avoid contradictions |
| Analogue gravity systems | 2-3 | Can model spacetime-like behavior in lab media | Analogue effects are not actual spacetime curvature |

## What Would Count as Strong Proof?

For OpenWarp, a strong proof of useful exotic stress-energy would need:

- Measurable energy density or stress tensor effect
- Reproducible experimental setup
- Independent confirmation
- Tunable control parameter
- Spatial shaping ability
- Clear distinction from electromagnetic artifact
- Scaling analysis
- Evidence of gravitational or spacetime relevance

## Current Working Hypothesis

The most realistic early path is not building a warp drive. It is proving a smaller statement:

> A controlled lab system can produce a tunable stress-energy configuration that is relevant to energy-condition violation research.

If that fails, the warp-drive path probably fails at the first gate.

If that succeeds, the next gate is spacetime coupling.
